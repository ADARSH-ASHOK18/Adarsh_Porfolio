About Me

Dedicated to fostering academic excellence and driving innovation in technology education

AA

Empowering the Next Generation of Technologists

I am Adarsh Ashok, an Assistant Professor at Parul University, Vadodara, India. With a deep passion for Computer Science and emerging technologies, I am dedicated to shaping the future of education and research.

My teaching journey spans multiple disciplines including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cyber Security, and Internet of Things. I believe in creating engaging, hands-on learning experiences that bridge theoretical knowledge with practical application.

Beyond the classroom, I am actively involved in research, publishing papers on cutting-edge topics in AI and data mining. I also mentor students in their academic projects, guiding them to develop real-world solutions that address contemporary challenges.

My commitment to innovation extends to organizing workshops, conducting training programs, and fostering a culture of continuous learning within the academic community.
